Online Challenges & Practices

Would you like to try some yoga?
I found a small sampling of challenges
that might help you ease into it.
I hope you find something you like!

1. Do You Yoga 30-day Yoga Challenge:

This yoga challenge consists of 30 days of yoga in 10-20 minute long online video sessions. You do the yoga at your own pace and convenience. These free online yoga classes are the perfect opportunity to get into yoga or take your practice to the next level! Check it out here!

2. 30 Day Yoga for Beginners with Fightmaster Yoga:

This challenge is perfect for the beginning yogi. You will learn how to practice yoga safely and how to use proper alignment. The yoga challenge starts easy and eventually becomes more challenging. Click here!

3. Yoga by Candace:

This is not a challenge but Candace has a yoga playlist for everybody and anybody to do anywhere at anytime. Here is her YouTube playlist!

4. Shape Magazine Strike a Pose 30 Day Yoga Challenge:

Each week, you'll master six poses, then put them all together for a #FridayFlow. You'll start with the basics, the move through core-strengthening poses, flexibility-enhancing shapes, and strength-building moves. Check it out here!

5. Home Yoga Practice: Ekhart Yoga

This is a collection of Yoga and Meditation videos to help inspire your home yoga practice! Here is the YouTube playlist!
